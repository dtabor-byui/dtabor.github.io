Our Mission:

Welcome to Salmon River Adventures. We are located in Riggins Idaho and are one of the premiere rafting services in the western states. We have nearly 100 years of rafting experience with guides that are profiecent in rafting and safty procedures.We are known for our outstanding safety record.

We invite you to join us on our next ride. Come see what all the fuss is about.
