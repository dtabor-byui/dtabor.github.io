Target Audience :


The target audience for this website will be primarily local users of varying age and economic background. The primary user will be interested in not only weather but local events and important weather-related information. Each user may be motivated by different outdoor activities but the primary target will also be interested in the community and the local events that could pertain to him/her.

Personas/ Senarios:


1. Local fisherman

When it rains up country the river rises, and the fishing is terrible. This individual would use the site to optimize their time on the river to the most likely time for success. Comparing the lunar cycles and the rain in the mountains and current water levels this user would be able to maximize the potential of their day on the river.

2. Local Produce Grower

In this environment with such a short growing season every growing day could need to be monitored to maximize growing times. A local grower (whether they be a commercial or private) will benefit from the basic information provided. A freezes could be foretold in advance and measures could be taken to protect early plants from a late frost. On the contrary the first frost in the fall is critical in time harvest and maximizing yield

3. Outdoors Enthusiast

This category covers a broad range of people, from the mountain biker, to the cross-country skier. Little league event planners and coaches of every imaginable event. The down hill skier and the rock climber would all be interested in the incoming weather, and events that they could participate in. Camping could be planned on fair weather weekends and dangerous mountain storms could be avoided.

4. The Casual Outdoors Person

This category would be a person that would need to know the weather for an event or a certain time period. Perhaps a wedding or a family reunion. Perhaps, a scoutmaster that is trying to plan a day of kite flying or bike riding. Maybe a young man that is trying to come up with a local event that would be fun for a date, or a young lady that is trying to decide if she wants to even go on a date to that event.
