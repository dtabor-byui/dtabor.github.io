Target Audience :


The target audience for this website will be primarily local users of varying age and economic background. The primary user will be interested in not only weather but local events and important weather related information. Each user may be motivated by different outdoor activities but the primary target will also be interested in the community and the local events that could pertain to him/her.

Personas:


1. Betty Henderson

Betty is retired and she is known for her home grown produce and her garden is the envy of the entire county. She uses the site to to monitor the rain fall and moisture that is anticipated so her dear plants receive exactly what they need to be their very best.

2. Bob Anderson

Bob is a welder and he works long hours getting his job done. He doesn't like to take time off for fishing unless he know what the weather is going to be like. Bob uses the site to make sure the time he spends is going to be a great day on the river. He watches the barometer and the lunar cycle and uses his knowledge of insects, hatches and the temperature to ensure he has the best day fishing, every day he takes off.

3. Brett Smith

Brett is a High School Teacher that loves the outdoors and has much of the summer to himself. He will often spend weeks in the mountains fishing and hiking. He uses the site to make long term preparations and to track patterns on particular mountain ranges. This information helps him to be prepared for early or late snows and for upcoming weather.

Scenarios:

1. Farm Scenario

It is hay harvest season and a farmer is about to do his first hay cutting. Once that hay is on the ground it has to be dried and baled before it gets rained on or the value goes way down the site would be helpful in this scenario to assist in knowing when to cut and when to wait.

2. Sports Scenario

Often, events are planned around the weather, a softball tournament, outdoor tennis matches, and golf tournaments. This site would be useful to assist an individual planning such an event to avoid inclement weather with the best information available.

3. Outdoors Man Scenario

Being an outdoors man comes with inclement weather, but being prepared for the weather you are going to experience allows hikers to only carry essential gear. I can help individuals avoid treacherous weather, and it can be helpful in knowing when to postpone an outing due to weather concerns.

4. Casual Outdoors Person Scenario

This is the individual that is needing to have an event outdoors and needs to know what to expect. Wedding and reunions, and gatherings of all sorts wound use this site as a resource to plan or cancel events to be sure their guests were not exposed to dangerous or inclement weather unnecessarily.

5. Member of the Community Scenario

This is the individual that would be interested in only the community portion of the site. They would be interested in events and the happenings in their own (and surrounding) communities. This may be a young man trying to find an event to take a date. Perhaps, a local group looking for a place to set up a booth, or a scout troop planning a fund raiser.
