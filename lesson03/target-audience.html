Target Audience :


The target audience for this website will be primarily local users of varying age and economic background. The primary user will be interested in not only weather but local events and important weather-related information. Each user may be motivated by different outdoor activities but the primary target will also be interested in the community and the local events that could pertain to him/her.

Personas:


1. Betty Henderson

Betty is retired and she is known for her home grown produce and her garden is the envy of the entire county. She uses the site to to monitor the rain fall and moisture that is anticipated so her dear plants recieve exactly what they need to be their very best.

2. Bob Anderson

Bob is a welder and he works long hours getting his job done. He doesn't like to take time off for fishing unless he know what the weather is going to be like. Bob uses the site to make sure the time he spends is going to be a great day on the river. He watches the barometer and the lunar cycle and uses his knowledged of insects hatches and the temperature to ensure he has the best day fishing, every day he takes off.

3. Brett Smith

Brett is a High School Teacher that loves the outdoors and has much of the summer to himself. He will often spend weeks in the mountians fishing and hiking. He uses the site to make long term prepretations and to track patterns on particular mountian ranges. This information helps him to be prepared for early or late snows and for upcoming weather.

Senarios:

1. Farm Senario

It is hay harvest season and a farmer is about to do his first hay cutting. Once that hay is on the ground it has to be dried and baled before it gets rained on or the value goes way down the site would be helpful in this senario to assist in knowing when to cut and when to wait.

1. Sports Senario

Often, events are planned around the weather, a softball tournment, outdoor tennis matches, and golf tournements. This site would be useful to assit an individual planning such an event to avoid inclimate weather with the best information available.

1. Outdoorsman Senario

Being an outdoorsman comes with inclimate weather, but being prepared for the weather you are going to experience allows hikers to only carry essential gear. I can help individuals avoid treachous weather, and it can be helpful in knowing when to postpone an outing due to weather concerns.

1. Casual outdoorsperson Senario

This is the individual that is needing to have an event outdoors and needs to know what to expect. Wedding and renunions, and gatherings of all sorts wound use this site as a resource to plan or cancel events to be sure thier guests were not exposed to dangerous or inclimate weather unnecessarily.
