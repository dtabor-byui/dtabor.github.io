The Bear Lake Blues ...

When the snows first begins to melt in Eastern Idaho nearly every individual is suffering with some form of "cabin fever". Every year I marvel at the early snows in December and then in April I am disgusted by the dirty piles that remain. At this time of year the soul longs to feel the sun and stretch the legs. The good news is that there is a rememdy for cabin fever and all we need to do is look to some of those activities in our community. Here is a link to a website with information about our community, so get out there and get rid of that winter fat!
