Use of Whitespace


Dell does a great job of highlighting the product that they are trying to sell by sorrounding it with white space. It makes the design seem more elegant and clean while drawing the users eye to the product.

An example of Fitts' Law


Here we see an example of Fitts' law. Fitts suggest that the larger an object is the easier it is for the human to move and then connect with that item. Boeing makes each link large so that the user can connect with each item easily.

An example of Hick's law


BHCH does a great job of limiting our choices here, and that is an example of Hick's law. Hicks suggest that the more choices wh have the more confused humans become. By simplfying the choices it allows the user to make simple choices they feel good about.
